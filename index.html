Step 1 — Your GSTIN

Your 15-character GST Identification Number

📍 Where to find your GSTIN?
It's on your GST registration certificate, or go to gst.gov.in → My Profile → GSTIN. It looks like: 29ABCDE1234F1Z5
15 characters: State code + PAN + Entity + Check digit
